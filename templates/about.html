About

The idea.

We have always believed that music plays a big role in defining one’s personality. Therefore, we wanted to make a project that helped encapsulate one’s personality and visualize it, and the perfect way to do so was through a wallpaper. If you are a regular mobile or computer user, this wallpaper is the first thing you would see when you wake your device, a regular reminder of who you are and where your interests lay. We give you the means of forming this wallpaper in your own personal ways whether it be through picking the time period of music you listened to, or drag & dropping album covers on the website to make the most aesthetic wallpaper possible. This wallpaper is completely up to you to design, and we are so honored to be part of that process with you. This project has been one of our proudest accomplishments. Sahil came up with the idea 3 years ago, and since then it has evolved from being a hackathon project, to a class project, to what it is today. Here’s to more updates to come and bringing even more meaning to this project!
